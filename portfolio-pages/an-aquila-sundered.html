Meghan Rennie

An Aquila Sundered

  • Medium: Graphic Design
  • Software used: Adobe Illustrator
  • Project Type: Commission
  • Year: 2021
1 / 1

Meghan was hired to create a logo for An Aquila Sundered, a mod of the video game Victoria 2. The logo would be used strictly digitally, mostly for icons and potentially on menu screens, meaning it had to be distinct and eye-catching at a small size.

The client proffered the logo for Kaiserreich (a similar game) as an aesthetic reference. They also stipulated that "the Carnyx smashing the Aquila must be included [in the design]."
