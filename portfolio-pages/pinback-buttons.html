Meghan loves the accessibility of pinback buttons. They're easy to disseminate; affordable to make and purchase; and, as a wearable accessory, they occupy a special artistic context. Buttons become a part of the wearer's curated identity. Furthermore, they become a type of public art, finding an audience in anyone whom the wearer meets throughout the day. All in all, what's not to love?

Meghan makes her buttons by hand with her own button press. They are currently for sale at the Allied Arts Council shop, located at 318 7 St S, Lethbridge
